Drug Info: PainAwayMax

PainAwayMax is a brand of ibuprofen manufactured by FooCorp . It is an analgesic in the class of nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory (NSAID) drugs. It is used to temporarily relieve minor aches and pains due to:
  • headache
  • toothache
  • backache
  • menstrual cramps
  • the common cold
  • muscular aches
  • minor pain of arthritis
and to temporarily reduce fever .
It is available in the US as a non-prescription drug in the form of tablets and capsules.
Common side effects include diarrhea , constipation , bloating , dizziness , or nervousness . Call a doctor immediately if you experience any of the following rare but serious side effects: vomiting blood , bloody stools , or stomach pain that does not get better .
Do not use PainAwayMax if you have ever had an allergic reaction to any other pain reliever/fever reducer , or right before or after heart surgery .
Warning: NSAIDs may cause severe stomach bleeding.
