A ProductModel gives a datasheet or vendor specification of a product (in the sense of a prototypical description). Use this type when you are describing a product datasheet rather than an actual product, e.g. if you are the manufacturer of the product and want to mark up your product specification pages. For example: The ACME Colorvision 123 specifiation on the ACME Corp. site for that model.
ACME Colorvision 123 The ACME Colorvision 123 is the leading-edge color TV from our company. EAN/UPC: 1234567890123
Recommendation: Try to provide identifiers (gtin13, gtin14, gtin8, mpn properties) and link to you as the manufacturer (manufacturer property) so that search engines can use your data to enrich offers of your products found elsewhere.
